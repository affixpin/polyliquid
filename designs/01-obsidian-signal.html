How It Works

Three layers of security

Delegation, consensus, and dispute resolution.

01

LP Delegation

LPs choose voters by track record and delegate capital. Commission rates: 5–80%. Higher reputation = lower risk for LPs.

02

Commit-Reveal Voting

Voters submit encrypted votes, then reveal. Weight = Stake × Rep. Consensus at 50% + 1. 99% of markets resolve in 8 hours.

03

DAO Dispute

Traders dispute with a 2% bond. DAO votes over 48h. Attackers lose everything. Correct voters are rewarded.
