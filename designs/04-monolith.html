How It Works

Three layers

1

LP Delegation

LPs choose voters by track record and delegate capital. Commission: 5–80%. Higher reputation means lower risk for LPs.

2

Commit-Reveal Voting

Voters submit encrypted votes, then reveal. Weight = Stake × Reputation. Consensus at 50% + 1. 99% resolve in 8 hours.

3

DAO Dispute

Traders dispute with a 2% bond. DAO votes over 48 hours. Attackers lose everything. Correct voters are rewarded.
