The Problem

Oracles are the weakest link

UMA uses token-weighted voting. A whale can purchase influence in a single transaction. The system has been exploited repeatedly — $7M in March 2025, $160M in July 2025.
How It Works

Three layers of security

Delegation, consensus, and dispute resolution.
1

LP Delegation

LPs choose voters by track record. Commission: 5–80%. Higher reputation means lower risk for LPs.

2

Commit-Reveal

Encrypted votes, then reveal. Weight = Stake × Rep. 99% of markets resolve in 8 hours.

3

DAO Dispute

2% bond to dispute. DAO votes 48h. Attackers lose everything. Correct voters are rewarded.
